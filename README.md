# Welcome in the Hyperreal world

A collection of observations and philosophy into the 21st century.
The term [Hyperreality](https://github.com/genboy/hyperreal/blob/master/hyperreality.md) was first used by Daniel Boorstin and [Jean Baudrillard](http://enterhyperreality.weebly.com/jean-baudrillard.html). In hyperreality, it is assumed that our approaches become detached from ideal forms, leaving our perceptions of reality based on other approaches, without the ability to restore the original true form. 

[Simulacra and Simulations- Jean Baudrillard](https://github.com/genboy/hyperreal/blob/master/sources/media/Baudrillard_Simulacra%20and%20Simulations.pdf)



Preface
I notice that many people think and say they don't care about whether something is real or not, where it really comes from, whether it is true and what it really means. We might be just looking to see if we can grasp something that feels true and give it value in our lives, or else we throw it away if we can so that it doesn't become part of our reality, at first. we think. Probably because reality is actually quite complicated. In such a complicated way, in fact, that a lot of falsehoods come across as truths, so that we have developed a habit of living with falsehoods. We actually need a sense of realness, a feeling of being alive that is the key to go on living, a feeling of oneness with life, not just a strange idea of separation of what is real and what is not and therefor good or bad. In the many ways we do confront our true urge to feel alive, it is extremely difficult to graps a sense of reality. Feeling alive and at peace might just be the opposite of our current state in reality.
