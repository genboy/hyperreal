# Welcome in the Hyperreal world

A collection of observations and philosophy into the 21st century.
The term [Hyperreality](https://github.com/genboy/hyperreal/blob/master/hyperreality.md) was first coined by [Jean Baudrillard](http://enterhyperreality.weebly.com/jean-baudrillard.html). In hyperreality, it is assumed that our approaches become detached from ideal forms, leaving our perceptions of reality based on other approaches, without the ability to restore the original true form. 


Preface
I notice that many people don't care about whether something is real or not, where it really comes from, whether it is true and what it really means. We just look to see if we can grasp it and give it value in our lives, or else we throw it away so that it doesn't become part of our reality, at first. we think. Probably because reality is actually quite complicated. In such a complicated way, in fact, that a lot of falsehoods come across as truths, so that we have developed a habit of living with falsehoods. And in a life made up of falsehoods it is extremely difficult to graps a sense of reality. In this way we can understand that many people assume no human can actually know what is real, the concept of reality will always question itself.
